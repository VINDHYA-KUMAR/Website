Archi Site Mobius




Archi Site Mobius (ASM) demonstrates how a well-crafted digital presence can elevate architectural practice in a competitive market. With a mission to create serene spaces, ASM’s website reflects both expertise and creativity. The homepage is thoughtfully designed, featuring the brand’s name, an about page, and a clear showcase of projects enhanced by next and previous navigation. Smooth GSAP animations bring the project exhibition to life, with clever transition effects that captivate visitors as they move from one project to another. Inner pages expand the experience by presenting more images and details, offering a deeper look into each architectural concept. Clean layouts, visual hierarchy, and interactive elements combine to deliver a user-friendly and visually engaging platform that mirrors ASM’s design philosophy.



Design Philosophy of Archi Site Mobius


1. Fluid spatial design – Continuous and flowing architectural forms


2. Experimental geometry – Use of complex curves and dynamic structures


3. Parametric thinking – Digital-driven design processes shaping form and space


4. Light-integrated forms – Openings and surfaces designed to guide light movement


5. Integration of structure and form – Architecture where structure becomes expression


6. Future-oriented design – Innovative concepts that challenge conventional architecture


7. Iconic visual identity – Buildings designed as sculptural landmarks
