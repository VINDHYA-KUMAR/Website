Amanda Martocchio




Amanda Martocchio Architecture (AMA) is driven by a mission to design environments that enrich lives and foster wellness. Whether creating new buildings or transforming existing structures, AMA crafts architecture that harmonizes with its surroundings while filling interior spaces with warmth and natural light. Their collaborative, iterative process ensures each design solution reflects the client’s personal aesthetics, functional needs, and budgetary goals. Beyond aesthetics and functionality, AMA embraces a broader responsibility—protecting the earth’s resources and reducing the carbon footprint. This commitment to sustainability, combined with a focus on human-centered design, defines AMA’s work as both inspiring and conscientious, offering spaces that elevate everyday living while respecting the planet.



Design Philosophy of Amanda Martocchio


1. Expressive minimalism – Soft, sculptural forms combined with restrained design


2. Material sensitivity – Use of natural materials and textured finishes


3. Warm modernism – Clean aesthetics balanced with warmth and comfort


4. Light-focused interiors – Natural light used to enhance form and mood


5. Subtle color palettes – Neutral tones with gentle contrasts


6. Craft and detail – Attention to bespoke elements and refined finishes


7. Calm, intimate spaces – Interiors designed to feel serene, personal, and inviting
